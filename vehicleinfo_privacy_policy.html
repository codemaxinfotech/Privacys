Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Introduction

RCCheck ("we", "our", or "us") operates the RCCheck mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Vehicle Registration Numbers: When you search for vehicle details, we process the registration number you enter to fetch information from public government databases.
  • Driving License Numbers: When you use the DL lookup feature, we process the DL number to fetch details from government databases.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Features used, search history within the app, time and date of access, and app performance data.
  • Log Data: In case of an error, we may collect log data including your device's IP address, device name, OS version, app configuration, time and date of usage, and other diagnostic data.

2.3 Cached Data

We store vehicle search results locally on your device for up to 2 hours to provide faster results and reduce network usage. This cached data is stored only on your device and is automatically deleted after the cache period expires.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the vehicle/DL lookup service
  • To cache search results locally for improved performance
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To analyze app usage and improve our services
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • To ensure app security and prevent misuse

4. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

4.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

4.2 Firebase

We use Firebase services for push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging) and remote configuration (Firebase Remote Config). Firebase may collect device identifiers and usage data. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

4.3 Government APIs

We do not have any association with any state RTO. All vehicle information about vehicle owners shown in the app are publicly available on the Parivahan/mParivahan website (https://parivahan.gov.in/parivahan/). We are only acting as an intermediary to make this information easily available to the users via the mobile app. We do not store this data on our servers beyond the local device cache.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Search results are cached locally on your device using an encrypted local database (Room) for up to 2 hours.
  • We use AES-256 encryption and native security layers to protect sensitive configuration data.
  • API communications are secured using HTTPS.
  • We do not store your personal data on external servers.
  • We implement security checks including VPN detection, root detection, and app integrity verification to protect against tampering and data interception.

6. Data Retention

  • Local Cache: Vehicle data is cached on your device for 2 hours, then automatically deleted.
  • Search History: Your recent searches are stored locally on your device. You can clear this at any time from the History screen.
  • Preferences: App settings (theme, language, onboarding status) are stored locally on your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local search history at any time
  • Opt out of personalized advertisements through device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Request information about what data we process

9. Advertising

Our App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob, including banner ads, native ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded video ads. These ads may be personalized based on your interests and device information. You can manage your ad preferences in your device's Google settings under "Ads".

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
