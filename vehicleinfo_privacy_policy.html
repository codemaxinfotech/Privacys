Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Introduction

RCCheck ("we", "our", or "us") operates the RCCheck mobile application (the "App"), available on Google Play Store under package name com.rccheck.vehicleinfo. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

Disclaimer: We do not have any association with any state RTO. All vehicle information about vehicle owners shown in the app are publicly available on the Parivahan/mParivahan website (https://parivahan.gov.in/parivahan/). We are only acting as an intermediary to make this information easily available to the users via the mobile app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Vehicle Registration Numbers: When you search for vehicle details, we process the registration number you enter to fetch information from public government databases.
  • Driving License Numbers: When you use the DL lookup feature, we process the DL number to fetch details from government databases.
  • Saved/Bookmarked Data: When you bookmark a vehicle or DL, the details are stored locally on your device for quick access.
  • App Preferences: Your chosen theme (light/dark/system) and language preference.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

The following data is collected automatically through third-party SDKs integrated in the App:

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personal identity information.
  • We do NOT collect your location (GPS, network, or approximate).
  • We do NOT collect photos, contacts, files, or any data from your device storage.
  • We do NOT require account creation, login, or registration of any kind.
  • We do NOT store any user data on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the vehicle RC and driving license lookup service
  • To store search results and bookmarks locally on your device for faster access
  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob (banner, native, interstitial, rewarded, and app open ads)
  • To monitor app stability and fix crashes via Firebase Crashlytics
  • To manage app configuration remotely via Firebase Remote Config
  • To ensure app security through VPN detection, root detection, and integrity checks

4. Data Storage

4.1 Local Storage (On Your Device Only)

All user data is stored exclusively on your device. We do NOT transmit or store any user data on external servers.

  • Search Cache: Vehicle and DL search results are cached permanently on your device using an encrypted local database (Room). Data older than 2 hours is considered stale and is automatically refreshed from the API on next access, but the local copy is always available for instant viewing.
  • Search History: Your RC and DL search history is stored locally. You can clear all history at any time from the History screen or Settings.
  • Saved/Bookmarked Items: Vehicles and DLs you bookmark are stored permanently on your device until you manually remove them.
  • App Preferences: Theme mode (light/dark/system), language selection, and onboarding status are stored locally via Android DataStore.

4.2 Data Security

  • All network communications are encrypted in transit using industry-standard encryption.
  • We implement multiple layers of security to protect the App and your data from unauthorized access and tampering.
  • Owner/holder names are partially masked by default to protect personal information of vehicle owners.

5. Third-Party Services

Our App integrates the following third-party services. Each service has its own privacy policy governing data collection:

5.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements including banner ads, native ads, interstitial ads, rewarded video ads, and app open ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your device settings.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by going to your device's Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.2 Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports, diagnostic data, and device information to identify and fix app issues. This data includes device model, OS version, crash stack traces, and custom log messages (such as last screen visited and last search performed).

Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5.3 Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App, including screen views and feature usage. This helps us improve the App experience.

5.4 Firebase Remote Config

We use Firebase Remote Config to remotely manage app settings such as ad unit IDs, ad display frequency, and feature flags without requiring an app update.

5.5 Firebase Cloud Messaging

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) for push notifications. FCM may collect device tokens for notification delivery.

5.6 Government Data Sources

Vehicle registration and driving license information is fetched from publicly available government databases (VAHAN/Parivahan/Sarathi portals maintained by the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways, Government of India). We are not affiliated with any government body. The information displayed is publicly available data.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Search Cache: Stored permanently on your device. Stale data (older than 2 hours) is refreshed automatically but never deleted. You can clear all cached data from Settings → Clear Cache.
  • Search History: Stored permanently on your device. You can clear all history from the History screen using the "Clear all history" button.
  • Saved/Bookmarked Items: Stored permanently until you manually remove them by tapping the remove button.
  • Crash Logs: Retained by Firebase Crashlytics for up to 90 days.
  • Complete Data Removal: Uninstalling the App will remove ALL locally stored data including cache, history, bookmarks, and preferences.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is designed for users aged 18 and above. The App is NOT directed at children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App displays personalized advertisements which are not suitable for children under the age of consent.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the App, please contact us and we will take steps to address your concerns.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local search history at any time from the History screen
  • Remove saved/bookmarked vehicles and DLs at any time from the Profile screen
  • Clear cached data from Settings → Clear Cache
  • Opt out of personalized advertisements through your device's Google Ads settings
  • Uninstall the App to completely remove all locally stored data
  • Contact us to request information about how your data is processed
